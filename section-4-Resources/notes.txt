Section 4: CloudFormation Resources

What are resources?
	-Resources are the core of your cloud formation template
	-They represent the different AWS components that will be created and configured
	-Resources are declared and can reference each other
	-AWS figures out creation, updates and deletes resources for us
	-There are over 224 types of resources
	-Resource type identifiers are of the form:
		“AWS::aws-product-name::dta-type-name”

How do I find resource documentation
	-Google the product name documentation
	-When you're in the designer, drag and drop the resource you want to use. Then right click on the resource and hit the documentation icon
